About

I am Andrew Inguanez, a 20-year-old Maltese artist and designer currently reading for a degree in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. My design interests include photography, fine art painting and analogue collaging. Themes often explored in my personal work are often centered around ideas of nature, human portraiture and landscape.

Digital competencies include Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and Lightroom. Creative skills included branding and logo design, photography, illustration, fine art painting and editorial design.
